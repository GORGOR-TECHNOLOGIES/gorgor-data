About Gorgor Aviation

For over three decades, we've been the premier institution for aviation training, producing some of the world's finest pilots and aviation professionals.

Your Journey to the Skies Starts Here

Founded in 1985, Gorgor Aviation College has been training world-class pilots and aviation professionals for over three decades. Our commitment to excellence, safety, and innovation has made us a leader in aviation education.

With state-of-the-art flight simulators, a modern fleet of training aircraft, and instructors with real-world experience, we provide an unparalleled learning environment for aspiring aviators.

Our Programs

We offer comprehensive aviation programs designed to meet industry standards and prepare you for a successful career in the skies.

Private Pilot License (PPL)

Start your aviation journey with our comprehensive PPL program. Learn the fundamentals of flying and earn your license to fly single-engine aircraft.

  • Minimum age: 17 years
  • 40 hours flight time
  • Ground school training
  • Solo flight experience
Duration 3-6 months
Cost $10,000
Program Details

Commercial Pilot License (CPL)

Take your skills to the professional level with our CPL program. Train to become a commercial airline pilot with advanced flight training.

  • Requires PPL certification
  • 250 hours flight time
  • Advanced flight maneuvers
  • Night flying training
Duration 12-18 months
Cost $35,000
Program Details

Flight Instructor Course (CFI)

Share your knowledge and passion for flying by becoming a certified flight instructor. Our CFI program prepares you to train the next generation of pilots.

  • Requires CPL certification
  • Instructional techniques
  • Flight training methodology
  • FAA certification preparation
Duration 6-9 months
Cost $15,000
Program Details
