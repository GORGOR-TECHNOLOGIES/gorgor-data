About Us

Welcome to Gorgor Aviation College

Welcome to Gorgor Aviation College, a premier institution dedicated to shaping the next generation of aviation professionals. Our college is committed to providing world-class education, hands-on training, and industry-relevant programs that prepare our students for a thriving career in aviation.

At Gorgor Aviation College, we offer specialized programs in Airport Operations, Flight Dispatch, Aviation Security, Airport Tower Operations, and Marshalling. Our curriculum is designed to balance theoretical knowledge with practical skills, ensuring graduates are fully equipped to meet the dynamic demands of the aviation industry.

Our state-of-the-art classrooms and training facilities include advanced equipment and simulators that replicate real-world aviation environments, allowing students to gain unparalleled hands-on experience.

Guided by a vision of excellence and a mission to empower students, Gorgor Aviation College fosters a culture of innovation, professionalism, and ethical practice. Whether you aspire to manage airport operations, ensure aviation security, or coordinate air traffic, we provide the education and support to help you soar to success.
